About Me

Hi, I’m Maryam SabirAli Datey, a passionate front-end web developer dedicated to crafting visually engaging, user-friendly websites. My passion lies in creating clean, user-friendly designs that look great and feel seamless to navigate, no matter the device. With a deep understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I also enjoy working with modern frameworks like React to build dynamic web applications.

Having completed a degree in Computer Application and built up hands-on experience in various projects, My goal as a front-end developer is not only to create websites that look beautiful but also to ensure they perform well and offer intuitive navigation. I’m all about writing clean, efficient, modular code and love the challenge of making something complex work simply. I also pay close attention to responsiveness and accessibility. In addition to front-end technologies, I am well-versed in version control with Git, and Figma for UI/UX design. ensuring that my skill set remains current and adaptable.

Feel free to check out my portfolio to see some of my recent work. I look forward to collaborating on exciting projects and helping bring your web ideas to life!
